FAQ

Clear answers for members, partners, and operators.

How do I sign up for Confession Bucket?
Create an account, choose a membership tier, and complete the onboarding ritual to unlock your profile and community access.
Can I control who sees my confession?
Yes. Each confession can be set to public, Flamekeeper-only, or private. Privacy defaults stay conservative by design.
How is Legacy Score calculated?
Legacy Score combines vulnerability depth, empathy given, journal consistency, mentorship actions, and healthy streaks in a weighted model.
How do I report harmful content?
Use the report action on any post or message. Safety Stewards review incidents through the moderation queue and escalate when needed.
Can partners view personal confessions?
No. Partner organizations only receive aggregate impact reporting. Private content stays private.
